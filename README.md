### 3. About Data
This dataset represents Meta Ads Performance Data, covering campaigns, ads, user demographics, and ad interaction events. It is modelled after how Facebook/Instagram (Meta) ad platforms capture data.<br>
The purpose of this dataset is to analyse advertising performance, optimize targeting, and measure ROI (Return on Investment) through KPIs such as:<br>
‚Ä¢	Impressions (how often ads are seen)<br>
‚Ä¢ 	Clicks (engagement with ads)<br>
‚Ä¢ 	Purchases (conversions)<br>
‚Ä¢	CPM, CPC, CTR, and ROAS (efficiency metrics)<br>
‚Ä¢ 	Audience insights (demographics, location, interests)<br>
This dataset is ideal for building a Power BI Dashboard to evaluate campaign effectiveness,
budget utilization, and user engagement patterns.

### 4. Highlights 

‚Ä¢	Business Problem:<br>
	Meta ad performance analysis typically addresses business problems related to maximizing return on investment (ROI), optimizing ad spend, and improving the efficiency of customer acquisition.<br>

‚Ä¢	Goal of Dashboard:<br>
	The dashboard's goal is to provide a centralized, real-time, and visual platform that addresses the above problems by:<br>
